Background

Barbara Schroeder

Barbara Schroeder is an award-winning designer and artist. Her experience allows her to work efficiently and use her expertise to create comfortable, functional, and appealing living spaces. As a native of Kearney, Nebraska she has lived and worked in several states. She is an American Society of Interior Design associate. She has completed countless commissioned artworks for clients. Her hand painted canvas rugs, paintings, drawings, and furniture can be found in many homes in Nebraska and around North America.
